DisneySea Japan

Tokyo DisneySea, located in Urayasu, Chiba (near Tokyo), is a unique, marine-themed Disney park designed for all ages, with a focus on higher-quality dining and romantic, adventurous atmosphere compared to Disneyland

Map and Location

More Information

Tokyo DisneySea generally operates from 9:00 a.m. to 9:00 p.m. daily.
Key Ticket Information:
Pricing Structure: Prices fluctuate based on weekends, holidays, and season, with higher prices generally on busier days.
Ticket Types (1-Day):
Adult (18+): ¥7,900 – ¥10,900
Junior (12-17): ¥6,600 – ¥9,000
Child (4-11): ¥4,700 – ¥5,600
Under 4: Free admission.
Where to Buy: Tickets are available online through the official Tokyo Disney Resort website, app, or authorized resellers like Klook Travel.

Facilities

Key Themed Ports and Top Attractions
-Fantasy Springs (New): Features Anna and Elsa’s Frozen Journey, Rapunzel’s Lantern Festival, and Peter Pan’s Never Land Adventure.
-Mysterious Island: Journey to the Center of the Earth (fast-paced thrill) and 20,000 Leagues Under the Sea (submarine adventure).
-American Waterfront: Toy Story Mania! (interactive 3D) and Tower of Terror (drop tower).
-Lost River Delta: Indiana Jones Adventure: Temple of the Crystal Skull and Raging Spirits (roller coaster).
-Port Discovery: Aquatopia (spinning water ride) and Nemo & Friends SeaRider.
-Arabian Coast: The Magic Lamp Theater (3D show).
-Mermaid Lagoon: Sindbad’s Storybook Voyage and various family-friendly rides.
-Mediterranean Harbor: Soaring: Fantastic Flight and Venetian Gondolas.
Key Facilities and Services
-Entertainment: The Big Band Beat show at Broadway Music Theatre.
-Dining & Shopping: Diverse themed restaurants like Royal Banquet of Arendelle.
-Services: Free water fountains are available throughout the park.
-Accessibility: Picnic areas are available outside the park for guests bringing their own food.
-Logistics: The park opens typically at 8:00 AM, with early arrival recommended.
