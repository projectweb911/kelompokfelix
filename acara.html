DisneySea Japan

Tokyo DisneySea hosts immersive, season-specific events, highlighted by the 25th Anniversary "Sparkling Jubilee" (starting April 2026) featuring global dance performances and, in 2025/2026, themed merchandise and food. Key seasonal events include "Summer Cool-off," "Disney Halloween," "Disney Christmas," and "Duffy and Friends" celebrations, featuring harbor shows, special decorations, and character appearances.

Osaka Castle

Osaka Castle Spring Festival, Mid Mar–Mid May, 2026 | Japan ...Osaka Castle hosts year-round, top-tier events, most notably the Spring Festival (mid-March to May) with 300+ blooming cherry trees and nightly illuminations, and the winter Osaka Castle Illumination (Nov-Feb) featuring millions of LED lights. Visitors can also enjoy historical museum exhibits, special castle illuminations, and vibrant food stalls within the expansive Osaka Castle Park.

Ghibli Museum

The Ghibli Museum in Mitaka, Japan, offers a magical, immersive experience featuring revolving special exhibitions (currently "The Studio of Yuriko Yamawaki" through May 2027), exclusive short films at the Saturn Theater, and permanent displays on animation techniques. Highlights include a rooftop Robot Soldier, a Cat Bus for children, and themed,, often hands-on, exhibits.
