DisneySea Japan

Tokyo DisneySea is a unique, sea-themed Disney park in Japan, appealing to both families and adults with its sophisticated atmosphere, intricate ports of call (Mediterranean Harbor, Mysterious Island, Arabian Coast, etc.), and focus on adventure, romance, and discovery rather than just fairy tales

Osaka Castle

Osaka Castle is a stunning, iconic landmark in Japan, famous for its imposing modern reconstruction featuring a multi-tiered tower with golden decorations, surrounded by massive stone walls, moats, and lush parklands, housing a museum about its rich history under Toyotomi Hideyoshi and offering panoramic city views from its observation deck.

Ghibli Museum

The Ghibli Museum in Mitaka, Tokyo, is a whimsical, fairytale-like museum dedicated to the art of animation and the magical world of Studio Ghibli films
