About Us

We are Group 3 (TIK). We created this website to help people and tourists who want to go on vacation, especially to Japan. Our goal is to provide simple and useful information about tourist destinations in Japan, so visitors can plan their trip more easily and enjoy their holiday. We hope this website can be a helpful guide for anyone who wants to explore Japan and discover new experiences.
